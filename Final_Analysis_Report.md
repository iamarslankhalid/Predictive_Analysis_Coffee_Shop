# Project Overview
This project involves cleaning and analyzing a dataset of coffee shop sales to determine the profitability of products and services. It includes identifying strategies to increase profit margins or mitigate losses based on the analysis.

# Step 1: Data Cleaning
## Objective
Review the dataset, identify and rectify inconsistencies, missing values, and duplicates, and ensure data integrity and consistency.

## Methodology
- Removed duplicates.
- Standardized data formats.
- Converted time columns.

## Conclusion
The dataset is cleaned and ready for analysis.

# Step 2: Profit/Loss Analysis
## Objective
Compare product sales, identify products/services generating profit and those incurring losses, and visualize the results.

## Methodology
- Grouped data by product.
- Calculated total sales and profit.
- Visualized sales.

## Conclusion
Identified top profitable and loss-making products.

# Step 3: Profit Increase Prediction
## Objective
Utilize predictive modeling techniques to forecast future profits and explore factors contributing to profitability.

## Methodology
- Prepared data.
- Trained linear regression model.
- Analyzed coefficients.
- Generated recommendations.

## Conclusion
Provided actionable recommendations to increase profit margins.

# Step 4: Loss Mitigation
## Objective
Analyze factors contributing to losses and develop strategies to minimize or eliminate losses.

## Methodology
- Analyzed product data.
- Generated loss mitigation strategies.

## Conclusion
Provided actionable steps to convert loss-making products into profitable ones.
